About dynmirror

Dynmirror is a service to promote the use of metalinks, and helping communities (large and small) to dynamically mirror their files.

We have started August 19th 2009 and currently have a functional core system and currently you can:

  • Search for files based on their name.
  • Search for files based on digest (MD5, SHA-1, SHA-256, or SHA-512).
  • Dynamically create metalinks for your site.

We are always working on getting more features, but our core system of allowing the easy creation of metalinks is up and running.
